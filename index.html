About Us

Echoes of Humanity is a youth-led initiative that blends music, anthropology, and linguistics to bring healing and cultural understanding to those in need—especially cancer patients. We believe sound carries not just emotion, but memory, identity, and connection across all cultures.

Projects

  • Healing Recitals: Live and virtual performances for hospitals and care centers.
  • Sonic Stories: A digital archive of songs, memories, and spoken word from diverse communities.
  • Culture & Sound Workshops: Educational sessions on the role of music and language in healing.
  • Voices of the World Podcast: Coming soon — sharing stories from musicians, patients, and scholars.
